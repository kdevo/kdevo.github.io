Bachelor Thesis

About one year after the my Bachelor graduation, I asked myself: Why shouldn’t other people have the choice to read it and profit from my work?

In my thesis, I analyzed how well the Ethereum Blockchain can be integrated into exisiting business processes of the FH Aachen’s (University of Applied Sciences) landscape.

As a business process execution engine, I used the Open-Source Camunda BPM and wrote an own web3-connector. The authentication solution is hardware-based, working like an NFC terminal empowered by a cost-effective Raspberry Pi Zero.
